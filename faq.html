Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during a power outage?

All Stackly installations include military-grade UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) batteries. The system will continue to operate autonomously for up to 72 hours. Critical data is cached locally on Edge devices and synced once the connection restores.

How secure is the AI video data?

Extremely secure. We use end-to-end AES-256 encryption. Furthermore, our Edge computing architecture means video is processed locally on the camera itself; only the metadata (like alerts) is sent to the cloud, preserving absolute privacy.

Can Stackly integrate with my existing cameras?

Yes. Our AI nodes can be retrofitted to most standard IP cameras, instantly upgrading your legacy system with deep learning capabilities without replacing the hardware.

What is the installation process like?

After an initial security assessment, our engineering team designs a blueprint. Installation is typically completed over a weekend or during non-business hours to ensure zero disruption to your daily operations.

Do you provide maintenance and support?

Yes, all Pro and Enterprise tiers include 24/7 priority support and proactive maintenance. Our system self-diagnoses hardware issues and automatically dispatches a technician before a device fails.
